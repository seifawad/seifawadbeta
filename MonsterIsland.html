Monster Island

Monster Island is a game that I made by my self in the 9th grade and will be released on steam.

Overview

This is Monster Island, I made this game entirely by myself in the 9th grade, this game is a bit more complicated, this is a survival game, you try to survive on an island full of monsters and haunted houses. You are being chased by these monsters, you are given 1 weapon in the beginning of the game, you can go look for more, which are usually in haunted houses. How long can you survive in that kind of world? If you would like to see the game

The Near Future

I will be creating a full on new AI replacing the old one because it's weak, I will also be adding a loading screen and and an Inventory system, minor crafting, and just a bag to keep your stuff in it. We will make sure that the Ai has a tracking system.
